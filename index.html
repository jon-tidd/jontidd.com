Project #1
"What if bedtime stories could actually build character — not just entertain?"

Bedtime Virtues

Early Preview
Generosity

Character isn't born — it's built, one story at a time. Aristotle called it hexis. This app pairs AI-generated stories rooted in the cardinal virtues with parent discussion guides to make each lesson stick.

16 classical virtues organized under Prudence, Justice, Courage, and Temperance. 57+ curated classics spanning 2,600 years from Aesop to C.S. Lewis. A Virtue Compass radar chart tracks your child's character growth across every trait. AI stories generated via Claude, each mapped to specific virtues with guided discussion prompts.

Project #2
"What if you could map an entire mind — every podcast, every YouTube video, every written word — and then talk to it?"

The Rudyard Library

An explorable 3D atlas of Rudyard Lynch's intellectual universe. Built from his complete corpus — every episode diarized, transcribed, and structured into a living knowledge graph you can navigate and interrogate.

3.6 million words across 483 episodes — diarized with Whisper, chunked into 8,168 semantic passages, and embedded in a vector database. 469 books and 53 thinkers extracted and cross-referenced. 5,213 claims identified, categorized, and mapped into a 3D galaxy built with Three.js. RoboRudyard: a RAG-powered AI that answers in his voice with source citations to exact episodes.
Explore the Library

Independent fan project. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with Rudyard Lynch or Whatifalthist.
Project #3
"What does it actually take to shave 14 strokes off your game in a single summer?"

The Tiger Method

Live · In Season
The Tiger Method
SHAVE 14 STROKES
16-week on-course plan · bogey golf by Sept 1
Week 7 / 16 — Sand & Bunker Play

A 16-week, on-course practice plan engineered to turn a struggling weekend golfer into a consistent bogey shooter by September 1 — built on the insight that your short game, not your swing, is where the strokes actually hide.

96 drills across 16 weeks, weighted toward putting & chipping — 40% of all strokes. A live pace tracker scores you against the calendar (on pace vs. behind) and counts down the days to your goal. Progress saves locally and syncs across phone, tablet & laptop through a lightweight cloud store. Pure HTML/CSS/JS — no build, no login.

Project #5
"What if you could screen thousands of real-world sites — and have an engine tell you exactly which ones to pursue first?"

Site Screener

Stealth

A geospatial screening engine for an early-stage venture, with a recommendation engine built in. It ingests public-records data, scores every candidate 0–100 against weighted criteria, and tells the team which sites to pursue first — ranked on an interactive map. (What it's screening for stays under wraps.)

Recommendation engine — eight weighted criteria roll up to a 0–100 fit score, hard disqualifiers auto-zero fatal flaws, and every site gets a verdict: pursue now, visit, watch, or skip. Interactive map with color-coded results · drill-down sub-score breakdowns · shared votes & notes for the founding team. A fast, no-dependency static app behind an access gate.
Stealth venture · live tool

Project #6
"At a 600-person family camp, how do you find the handful of families you'll genuinely click with?"

Tyler Place Connections

Seasonal

A family-matching and search engine for guests at Tyler Place Family Resort. Tell it about your crew, and it surfaces your five best-matched families — then lets you explore the whole week on one map.

Match scoring across kids' ages, home states, interests & repeat-guest history · your top-5 connections computed instantly · all 74 families searchable & sortable · a full-roster connection map · year-over-year changes. A static app, password-gated for the community.
Built for the 2026 season · invite only
