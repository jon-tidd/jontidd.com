Bedtime Virtues

Early Preview

Aristotle called it hexis — the idea that virtue isn't something you're born with, but a habit forged through deliberate practice. For 2,600 years, the greatest thinkers agreed: character is built one story, one conversation, one bedtime at a time. This app brings that ancient insight to modern families with AI-generated stories rooted in the cardinal virtues — each paired with a parent discussion guide to make the lesson stick.

16 classical virtues organized under Prudence, Justice, Courage, and Temperance. 57+ curated classics spanning 2,600 years from Aesop to C.S. Lewis. A Virtue Compass radar chart tracks your child's character growth across every trait. AI stories generated via Claude, each mapped to specific virtues with guided discussion prompts.

The Rudyard Library

An explorable 3D atlas of Rudyard Lynch's intellectual universe. Built from his complete corpus — every episode diarized, transcribed, and structured into a living knowledge graph you can navigate and interrogate.

3.6 million words across 483 episodes — diarized with Whisper, chunked into 8,168 semantic passages, and embedded in a vector database. 469 books and 53 thinkers extracted and cross-referenced. 5,213 claims identified, categorized, and mapped into a 3D galaxy built with Three.js. RoboRudyard: a RAG-powered AI that answers in his voice with source citations to exact episodes.
