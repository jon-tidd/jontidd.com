IGCE Fit score (0–100): weighted match to the property-scanner brief — radiation-vault structural suitability (20), building size 15–30k SF ideal (15), lot/parking (15), highway access to I-95/395/495 (15), zoning (10), construction era (10), surroundings (8), off-market signal (7). Hard disqualifiers force 0 (usable <10k, vault-fail with no lot for an addition, lot <1ac & parking <60, >3mi from an interchange & >5mi from centroid, residential zoning). Bands: 80+ cold-call priority · 65–79 site visit · 50–64 watch · <50 skip. Proxy caveats: vault, construction era, and off-market are inferred from use-category/zone/stories/sale-date (no year-built, ceiling-height, floor-loading, or listing feeds in this dataset), and highway distance is straight-line to approximate interstate routes — treat as a triage rank, then verify. Where research found real facts (year built, stories, clear height, owner, surface-space counts, active listings, environmental red flags), they override the proxies — cards show them as '(researched)'/⚠ rows. Ranks 1–36 were adversarially validated against listings and property records, and 331 of 521 records carry researched facts (current as of July 2026 — listing status decays in weeks; verify before outreach). The Avail. column shows the deal path: For sale / Listed / Off-market. Hover any score for its top +/− factors. Scope: Fairfax County + City of Alexandria. Arlington's in-boundary sliver and the Prince William (Woodbridge) tail are excluded (low yield for freestanding flex, thinner open data). Business / owner: populated for Alexandria from the city assessor's property name; Fairfax withholds owner names in open GIS, so those show a "Who's here?" lookup link (enable Places API on the key and these can auto-fill). Data: Fairfax County GIS & City of Alexandria GIS — parcels, assessed values, zoning, footprints; Fairfax adds sales history, Alexandria adds stories. Building SF is footprint area. Parking est = (lot − footprint) ÷ 325 sf/space; verify by aerial. Footprint layers refresh on multi-year cycles. Not a substitute for survey, title, or zoning confirmation. The password gate here is a light client-side lock; a server-side lock is set at the host.
