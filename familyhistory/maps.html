The deep-time journey (100,000 BCE to 1273) follows the population-genetics and archaeology shared by everyone of English descent. Present-day counts come from Forebears surname-distribution data; the routes and figures for the eras in between are reconstructed from genealogical and census patterns. The shapes of the migration are real. Exact counts for, say, 1850 are informed approximations. See Sources.
