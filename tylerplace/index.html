How the match score works, in plain terms. For each family, their kids are paired with yours and each kid can only be claimed once, so a family with multiple well-matched kids scores far higher than one where just one kid happens to be close in age. A shared camp group is worth the most, a neighboring group nearly as much, then age gaps from there. Living nearby adds points, as does being at the same family stage. All data comes from the guest roster: names, hometowns, kids' ages and camp groups.
